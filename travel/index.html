Traveling to HackRice

If you've been accepted to HackRice from out of Rice, here is what you need to know about planning your travel. We're very excited to see you here!

Logistics

Date + Time

You should look to arrive by 5:15 PM, January 15th to either IAH or HOU. Check-in begins at 6:00 PM sharp. The hackathon ends at 3:00 PM, January 17th, so please keep that in mind when scheduling your return flights.

Getting Here

Splitting an Uber is probably the most efficient way to get to campus.

HackRice is located at Rice Memorial Center, Rice University, Houston, TX (also known as the Ley Student Center or RMC).

Flights

We'll be reimbursing based off what city you're flying out of. If your city is not listed, you can email us at officialhackrice@gmail.com to find out if you're eligible to be reimbursed.

Buses

HackRice Buses

We'll be sending buses to UT Austin, Texas A&M, UT Dallas, UT Rio Grande Valley, and UT San Antonio. A campus rep will contact you with more info about meeting location and departure time.

Megabus

If you choose to take a Megabus, then we can reimburse up to $20 of your fare. Please provide your travel receipts to officialhackrice@gmail.com, with the subject 'Bus Reimbursement'.

Driving + Parking

If you are driving, we'll provide parking passes for you for West and Greenbriar Lots. Check out Rice's campus map .
